- Hidden YouTube short transcript posts (`type: video`, `hidden: true`)

## Non-negotiable rules

1. Preview locally and share the URL before committing.
2. Do not commit until the author approves the preview.
3. Do not push or open a PR until the author explicitly asks.
4. One git worktree per post — never mix unrelated post work on one branch.
